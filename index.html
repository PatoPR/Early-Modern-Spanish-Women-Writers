MODERNAS

Spanish Women Writers from Early Modernity

Welcome to our website dedicated to exploring and celebrating the rich literary heritage of Spanish women writers from early modernity. Our mission is to bring to light the often-overlooked contributions of these remarkable women, including María de Zayas y Sotomayor, María Egual, Leonor de Meneses Noronha, Mariana de Carvajal y Saavedra, and Ana Francisca Abarca de Bolea y Mur. These writers defied the gender roles of their time and broke through the barriers that held them back, leaving behind a legacy of powerful, insightful, and influential literary works that continue to inspire and captivate readers today.
