Modernas Project

Early Modern Spanish Novella by Women

Welcome to our website dedicated to exploring and celebrating the rich literary heritage of Spanish women writers from early modernity.

Our mission is to bring to light the often-overlooked contributions of these remarkable women, including María de Zayas y Sotomayor, María Egual, Leonor de Meneses Noronha, Mariana de Carvajal y Saavedra, and Ana Francisca Abarca de Bolea y Mur. These writers defied the gender roles of their time and broke through the barriers that held them back, leaving behind a legacy of powerful, insightful, and influential literary works that continue to inspire and captivate readers today.

On this website, you’ll find in-depth research, insightful analysis, and fascinating historical context about the lives and works of these trailblazing writers. Our team of dedicated researchers is committed to uncovering the hidden gems of Spanish women’s literature, and we invite you to join us on this journey of discovery.
