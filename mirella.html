    Mirella Reichenbach Livotti

    Mirella Reichenbach Livotti is a Master´s student in the Hispanic Studies Program at the University of British Columbia (UBC). She also completed her undergraduate studies with a Double Major in Sociology and Latin American Studies from the same university. Her research interest is centred primarily on the intersection of gender and race in contemporary Latin American literature. In addition, she is interested in the representations of gender-based violence and its connection to work being done by feminist social movements and intellectuals. For this project, Re-imagining the Hispanic Novella, Mirella is responsible for creating a critical annotated bibliography of the author María Egual and her novel El esclavo de su dama.
